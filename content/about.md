# About

Hey, I'm **Ahmad Hassan** ([@ahmad9059](https://github.com/ahmad9059)), a Software Engineer and Full Stack Developer from Multan, Pakistan. I currently work at [VieroMind](https://vieromind.com) as an Associate Software Engineer and study Computer Science at MNS University of Agriculture, Multan (graduating 2027).

I build web applications, contribute to open source, and write about things I learn along the way. My focus areas include full-stack development with TypeScript and Next.js, cloud infrastructure with AWS, and AI-powered applications using Google Gemini and RAG pipelines.

## Work Experience

At **VieroMind Foundation, Inc.**, I work on AI-powered mental health and wellness tools. My day-to-day involves building scalable interfaces with Next.js and TypeScript, writing backend services in Node.js and Golang, and integrating machine learning models for journaling, mood tracking, and wellness insights. I received a full-time offer based on my performance during the Pakathon hackathon, where our team secured 2nd place.

## Open Source & Projects

My most well-known project is [**HyprFlux**](https://hyprflux.dev), a collection of Hyprland dotfiles and configs for a minimal, productive Arch Linux desktop. It has earned over 800 stars on GitHub, and I actively maintain it for the Hyprland community.

Other projects I've built include:

- [**SehatScan**](https://sehatscan.vercel.app): AI health assessment platform using multi-modal analysis with Google Gemini, Python, and AWS
- [**HisaabScore**](https://hisaabscore.vercel.app): AI-powered alternative credit scoring system for unbanked gig workers
- [**RAF-SP**](https://raf-sp.vercel.app): Agricultural data management system for the South Punjab agriculture department
- [**UAM Tracker**](https://uam-tracker.vercel.app): GPA/CGPA tracker for university students
- [**MindOasis**](https://mindoasis.vercel.app): Hackathon project helping users in Pakistan find mental health professionals

You can see all of them on my [projects page](/projects/).
